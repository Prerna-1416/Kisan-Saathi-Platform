Kisan Saathi Platform

Kisan Saathi is created to support anyone involved in farming by bringing important agriculture-related information together in one place. It helps with crop guidance, updated market prices, and clear information about government schemes so the right decisions can be taken at the right time. The purpose of Kisan Saathi is to make farming simpler, more reliable, and more beneficial for everyone.

Rice Farming

Rice is a staple crop grown during the monsoon season and supports food security.

View Crop Details

  • Soil Type: Clayey / Alluvial

  • Uses: Food grain, rice products
